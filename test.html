Key Objectives

  • To understand the current situation
  • To understand how to adjust our behavior at work
  • To understand how to adjust our behavior at home
  • To understand how to interact with guests regarding the water crisis

Outcomes:

  • A move from panic to action, gloom and doom to ‘let’s do it together’ attitude
  • Specific and practical action points that can be implemented at work to ensure maximum water is saved
  • Specific and practical action points that can be implemented in the home environment to save water
  • Role play and scenario planning conversations that may occur with guests, and ensuring that the right message is put across
